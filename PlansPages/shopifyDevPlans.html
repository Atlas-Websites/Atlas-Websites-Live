Why Do I Need A Maintenance Plan?

The key reason you need a maintenance plan for your Shopify Store is to keep your Shopify website online. So your online store can be kept secure and updated regularly. So we can provide you with our technical support for any of your issues. Your plan will pay the fees that keep the store online and running smoothly every month. The Maintenance plans also have a long list of features that will enhance your online store and provide analytics for you.

The monthly bills shown above are estimates, as you may have more requirements for each price plan. For example, you might want to have extra business emails.

A website is a long term investment, which with a Shopify Store the store requires monthly payments to keep everything working as it should be. So customers can continue to visit your online store and purchase your products.
