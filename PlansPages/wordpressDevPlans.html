Why Do I Need A Maintenance Plan?

The key reason you need a maintenance plan is to keep your website online. So your website can be secure and regularly updated. Your plan will pay the fees that keep the website online and running smoothly every month. That's why your visitors can still find your website. Maintenance plans also have a long list of features that will enhance your website and provide analytics for you.

The monthly bills shown above are estimates, since you may have more requirements for each price plan. You might want to have extra business emails, for example.

You don't need to have a maintenance plan. You can choose to pay an hourly rate to update your website on a monthly basis. So we can keep it online. A website is a long term investment, so the best deal for you is to choose a maintenance plan. In the long run, the hourly rate is going to be more expensive. If you plan to have a website in the long term, it is recommended that you have a maintenance plan.
